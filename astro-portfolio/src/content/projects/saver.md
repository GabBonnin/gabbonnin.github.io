## Overview

SAVER is a multi-center research infrastructure project within the German Center for Mental Health (DZPG), building one of the first large-scale databases of psychotherapy session recordings in German-speaking research settings.

## Objectives

- Build a standardized, privacy-preserving dataset of psychotherapy sessions across DZPG sites
- Develop and validate methods for responsible anonymization of audio data
- Study relationships between speech/language features and:
  - Clinical symptom trajectories
  - Therapeutic techniques and adherence
  - Patient motivation and engagement
  - Therapeutic alliance

## Innovation

A central focus of SAVER is the development of **responsible anonymization methods** that allow privacy-preserving speech data research while protecting patient confidentiality – a critical challenge in clinical NLP research.
