## Overview

POSIDONIA is a multi-center, adaptive randomized controlled trial (RCT) embedded within the German Center for Mental Health (DZPG), testing whether personalized, modular psychotherapy outperforms standard Behavioral Activation for patients with treatment-resistant depression.

## Design

- **Design**: Sequential Bayesian randomized parallel-group trial
- **Comparison**: Personalised Modular Psychotherapy vs. Behavioral Activation (BA)
- **Primary Endpoint**: Depression severity at Week 6 (Montgomery-Åsberg Depression Rating Scale, MADRS)
- **Sites**: Six DZPG sites across Germany
- **Population**: Adults with treatment-resistant depression

## Significance

POSIDONIA aims to:
1. Provide evidence for personalised, modular approaches in treatment-resistant depression
2. Establish a reusable adaptive trial infrastructure across DZPG's research network
3. Inform updates to clinical guidelines for difficult-to-treat depression
