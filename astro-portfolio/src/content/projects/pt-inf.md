## Overview

PT-INF is one of twelve cross-site research infrastructures within the German Center for Mental Health (DZPG), providing the backbone for systematic, harmonized data collection across all DZPG partner institutions.

## Objectives

Phase 1 establishes:
- A **network of institutions** committed to routine outcome monitoring
- A **secure, federated data platform** meeting GDPR and medical data protection standards
- **Standardized outcome measures** agreed upon across sites (e.g., PHQ-9, GAD-7, OQ-45)
- Data governance frameworks for multi-site data sharing

## Long-term Vision

PT-INF will create one of Europe's largest practice research networks (PRNs) for psychotherapy, enabling:
- Naturalistic effectiveness research at scale
- Identification of treatment moderators and predictors in real-world populations
- Benchmarking of outcomes across different care settings
- Infrastructure for embedding trials within routine care (pragmatic RCTs)
