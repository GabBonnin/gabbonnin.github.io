## Overview

DISKO developed and evaluated blended-learning programs for training mental health students in conducting structured diagnostic interviews for mental disorders — a critical competency that is often poorly taught in traditional academic settings.

## Programs

Two programs were developed:
1. **DISKO-Adult**: Training in DIPS-1.2-OA (Diagnostisches Interview bei psychischen Störungen – adults)
2. **DISKO-Child**: Training in Kinder-DIPS-OA (child and adolescent version)

Both combine:
- Asynchronous online lessons (video lectures, worked examples)
- Synchronous instructor-led practice sessions
- Structured feedback components

Both are **freely available via Moodle** across German universities.

## Evidence Base

A multi-center cluster-randomized non-inferiority trial (N = 350 students across multiple universities) demonstrated that DISKO:
- Produced **superior diagnostic knowledge** compared to traditional teaching
- Produced **superior practical skills** compared to traditional teaching
- Was non-inferior to in-person training while offering greater scalability

## Publication

Results published in the *Journal of Medical Internet Research* (2024). DOI: [10.2196/54176](https://doi.org/10.2196/54176)
