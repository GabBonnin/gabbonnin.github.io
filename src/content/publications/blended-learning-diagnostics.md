abstract: "This multicenter cluster-randomized noninferiority trial evaluated a blended-learning program (DISKO) for teaching structured diagnostic interviews for mental disorders. With 350 students across multiple German universities, the study demonstrated that the blended-learning approach produced superior diagnostic knowledge and practical skills compared to traditional teaching formats."
