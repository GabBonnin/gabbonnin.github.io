## Overview

POSIDONIA is a multi-center sequential Bayesian randomized parallel-group trial within the German Center for Mental Health (DZPG). It tests whether personalised modular psychotherapy outperforms Behavioral Activation as an active control condition for patients with treatment-resistant depression.
