## Overview

SAVER is a multi-center database project within the German Center for Mental Health (DZPG), building one of the first large-scale corpora of psychotherapy session recordings in German-speaking research settings. By combining audio recordings with diagnostic interviews and self-report outcome measures, SAVER creates a unique resource for studying the relationship between language, therapeutic processes, and clinical outcomes.
